how we got started.


three rising sophomores at harvard college decided to make paired for their cs50 final project. inspired by a comment in a harvard college facebook group, we bring to you a seamless, simple experience to get your work done effectively.


how paired works.


you can simply enter contact information, course department and desired course, and available times. you will then be greeted with a list of people who match your course and availability. if they approve your request to get in contact, our website exchanges your contact information with one another and leave the rest to you to set up a time to meet!
